Privacy Policy

For Lumos - The Magic Flashlight

This Privacy Policy has been effective from November 22, 2024

Please read the Privacy Policy before using the application.

Automatic data collection

When you use the application, it may automatically collect certain data. This data may include:

  • Ip Address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Your language
  • Voice recording for voice recognition

How do we use your data

At the moment we only use the language information to automatically display the application in your language.

How do we store your data

Your data is stored on the device you are using the application on. We do not store your data on our servers.

Third party services

For speech recognition we use the default android speech recognizer API (android.speech.speechrecognizer) provided by google.

Why are different permissions need?

For the application to work properly, it needs the following permissions:

  • Microphone - for voice recognition
  • Internet connection - for voice recognition
  • Camera - for flashlight. Unfortunately, the camera permission is needed for the flashlight to work. We do not use the camera in any way.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change the Privacy Policy at any time. The changes will be effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
